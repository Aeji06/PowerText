Auto Text Expander Options

Edit your database of shortcuts and expansions here! Some tips:

  • Shortcuts are case sensitive!
  • Shortcuts and expanded text cannot be blank.
  • Invalid shortcuts / expanded text will be highlighted red and not be saved.
  • Add a space to the end of your shortcut if you want it to trigger after typing a space.
  • Shortcuts should trigger in any textbox / input field on a page.
  • Shortcuts will not trigger in this options page.

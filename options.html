Auto Text Expander Options Feedback

Edit your database of shortcuts and expansions here! Would love your feedback on the Chrome Webstore page (link in upper right). Some usage tips:

  • Shortcuts and expanded text are case sensitive!
  • Date/time macros are now supported! We use Moment.js to do this; all you have to do is wrap your date format with %d(dateFormat). Please see this for how to format dates to work in your expanded text. An example expanded text could look something like: %d(MMMM Do YYYY), and would spit out something like: September 4rd 2013.
  • Add a space to the end of your shortcut if you want it to trigger after typing a space. Line breaks are supported in expanded text now!
  • Shortcuts should trigger in any textarea / input field on pages that let the extension load, indicated by the (txt)^r icon in the address bar.
  • Saving should sync across all Chrome browsers you sync with. New to Auto Text Expander? We'll provide you with some examples!
